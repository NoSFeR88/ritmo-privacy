Account & Data Deletion Request

Last Updated: November 11, 2025

1. Overview

At RITMO, we respect your privacy and provide you with the right to request the deletion of your account and any associated data at any time. This page explains how you can request data deletion and what happens when you do.

2. What Will Be Deleted

Upon confirmation of your request, the following information will be permanently removed:

  • Your user account and authentication data (email, name if applicable)
  • Your habits, routines, and progress data stored in the app
  • All synchronized data stored in Firebase (for Premium users)
  • Any crash logs or analytics data linked to your account

Data that is anonymized or aggregated (and no longer personally identifiable) may be retained for analytical purposes.

4. Processing Time

Once we receive your verified request, your account and associated data will be permanently deleted from our systems within 7 business days. You will receive a confirmation email once the deletion is complete.

5. Important Notes

  • Deleting your account is irreversible. All your habits, progress, and statistics will be lost permanently.
  • If you have a Premium subscription, please cancel it in Google Play before requesting deletion to avoid future charges.
  • Some logs or backups may persist up to 30 days in secure encrypted backups before being automatically purged.

RITMO values your privacy and is committed to handling your data with transparency and respect.
