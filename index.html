Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 6, 2025

TL;DR: RITMO respects your privacy. Your habit data stays on YOUR device. We don't sell your data. We don't show ads. We use minimal analytics to improve the app. You're in control.

1. Introduction

Welcome to RITMO ("we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application RITMO - Flexible Habit Tracker (the "App").

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Data You Provide

  • Account Information: If you choose to sign in with Google, we receive your name, email address, and profile picture from Google. This is optional.
  • Habit Data: Information about the habits you create, including habit names, descriptions, frequencies, completion records, and reminders.
  • App Settings: Your preferences such as theme selection, notification settings, and language.

2.2 Automatically Collected Data

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, unique device identifiers (for analytics only).
  • Usage Data: App interactions, features used, session duration (via Firebase Analytics - anonymized).
  • Crash Reports: If the app crashes, diagnostic information is sent to help us fix bugs (via Firebase Crashlytics).

2.3 Data We DON'T Collect

  • ❌ Location data
  • ❌ Contacts or phonebook
  • ❌ Photos, videos, or files
  • ❌ Microphone or camera access
  • ❌ Financial information (payment processing handled by Google Play)
  • ❌ Browsing history

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected data for:

  • App Functionality: To provide core features like habit tracking, reminders, gamification, and syncing (if enabled).
  • Improvement: To analyze app usage and fix bugs (anonymized analytics).
  • Communication: To send important updates about the App (only if you opt-in).
  • Legal Compliance: To comply with applicable laws and regulations.
We DO NOT:
  • ❌ Sell your data to third parties
  • ❌ Use your data for advertising
  • ❌ Share your habit data with anyone without your consent

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Local Storage (Primary)

By default, all your habit data is stored locally on your device using SQLite database. This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly enable cloud sync.

4.2 Cloud Storage (Optional)

If you enable cloud sync (Premium feature), your habit data is encrypted and stored in Firebase Firestore (Google Cloud). This allows you to access your data across multiple devices.

4.3 Security Measures

  • Data in transit: Encrypted using HTTPS/TLS
  • Data at rest: Stored in secure Firebase infrastructure with encryption
  • Access controls: Only you can access your habit data
  • No plaintext passwords: We use Google Sign-In (OAuth 2.0)

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may collect data:

5.1 Firebase (Google)

  • Firebase Analytics: Collects anonymized usage data to improve the app.
  • Firebase Crashlytics: Collects crash reports to fix bugs.
  • Firebase Authentication: Handles Google Sign-In securely.
  • Firebase Firestore: Stores habit data if cloud sync is enabled.

Firebase Privacy Policy: https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy

5.2 Google Play Services

Used for in-app purchases (Premium subscription). Payment processing is handled entirely by Google Play.

Google Play Privacy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

6. Your Rights and Choices

6.1 Access and Control

  • View Your Data: All your habit data is visible in the app.
  • Edit Your Data: You can modify or delete any habit at any time.
  • Export Your Data: You can export your habits as CSV/JSON (Settings > Data Export).
  • Delete Your Account: Settings > Account > Delete Account (permanently removes all cloud data).

6.2 Opt-Out Options

  • Analytics: You can disable analytics in Settings > Privacy > Analytics.
  • Notifications: Disable in-app or via Android Settings > Apps > RITMO > Notifications.
  • Cloud Sync: Disable in Settings > Premium > Cloud Sync.

6.3 GDPR Rights (EU Users)

If you're in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Rectify inaccurate data
  • Request deletion (right to be forgotten)
  • Object to processing
  • Data portability
  • Withdraw consent

Contact us at privacy@ritmoapp.com to exercise these rights.

6.4 CCPA Rights (California Users)

California residents have the right to:

  • Know what personal data is collected
  • Know if data is sold or disclosed (we don't sell data)
  • Request deletion
  • Opt-out of data sales (not applicable - we don't sell data)

7. Children's Privacy

RITMO is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If you are a parent/guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal data, please contact us at privacy@ritmoapp.com.

8. Data Retention

  • Local Data: Retained until you uninstall the app or clear data.
  • Cloud Data: Retained until you delete your account or request deletion.
  • Analytics: Anonymized data retained for up to 14 months (Firebase default).
  • Crash Reports: Retained for 90 days.

9. International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred to and processed in countries outside your country of residence (e.g., Google Cloud servers). We ensure adequate safeguards are in place (Firebase complies with GDPR, CCPA, and other frameworks).

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Posting the new Privacy Policy in the app
  • Updating the "Last updated" date at the top
  • Sending an in-app notification (for significant changes)

You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

11. Contact Us

If you have questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

  • Email: privacy@ritmoapp.com
  • Support: support@ritmoapp.com
  • App: Settings > Help & Support > Contact Us

12. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

For EU users, our legal basis for processing your data:

  • Contract: Processing necessary to provide the App services you requested.
  • Consent: You explicitly opt-in to optional features (cloud sync, analytics).
  • Legitimate Interest: Improving the app, fixing bugs, preventing fraud.

13. Cookies and Tracking

RITMO does not use cookies. We use Firebase Analytics for app usage tracking, which does not rely on traditional web cookies.

Why: To make the app work and improve it.

Who we share with: Nobody, except Google (for cloud sync if you enable it).

Your control: You can view, edit, export, or delete your data anytime.
